Atlas Orion

Atlas Orion is an two-in-one platform which showcases two major features. One is Atlas-Kanban which keeps track of all your tasks separated by boards. The other is Atlas-SSR which generates a server-side rendered about page for you.

Developed and maintained by Atlas Inc.

Why the name "Orion"?

Orion is a prominent constellation located on the celestial equator and visible throughout the world. It is one of the most conspicuous and recognizable constellations in the night sky. It is named after Orion, a hunter in Greek mythology. Its brightest stars are blue-white Rigel (Beta Orionis), which signifies the Atlas-Kanban feature and red Betelgeuse (Alpha Orionis), which signifies the Atlas-SSR feature.
